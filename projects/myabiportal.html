PROJECT DESCRIPTION

myabiportal.com is a B2B e-commerce & services portal which brings AB InBev's Europe zone customers online! Users can easily order beer and other products, manage financials (invoices, payments and bonuses etc.) and get help on any other question. The biggest beer brands collection and smooth UX on the portal make it a great tool for business owners and managers.

DESIGN PROCESS

Task-Request - Research - Ideation - Prototype - Feedback - Polish - QA - Release - Analyze

Design process is a non-linear process. It is iterative and really agile.

Motor Vehicle Registration
A Government Platform

Role: Senior UI/UX Designer

Skills: Mock-ups, UX, Design System, Prototypes

Period: Apr 2023 - Jul 2024

Contractor: Develit AB

CHALLENGE

Designed a Nationwide Vehicle Registration Platform from Scratch

PROJECT DESCRIPTION

This is a government platform which makes it possible to manage the whole life cycle of any motor vehicle within a country.

WHAT'S BEEN DONE

Year 2023

First year of the project, I was involved in creating the foundation and lots of MVPs:

- Motor Vehicle Verification - a service to check vehicles and put them into DB;

- Motor Vehicle Registration - a service to enroll vehicles, people and organizations to the system, then its full life-cycle management and services;

- Stock Control (Warehouse Management System) - 'Orders' component of the system, to manage stock and transfer/deliver products. Including a service for a whole chain of manager-picker-packer-deliver control and cooperation.

- work on Design System etc.

Year 2024

Second year of the project, I was involved in tuning, polishing main features and creating new ones:

- Motor Vehicle Verification - rework of the 'Form' (main feature of the service). Photo/file Uploader improvements;

- Motor Vehicle Registration - 'Booking' feature UI/UX improvements based on requested changes;

- Stock Control (Warehouse Management System) - 'Order Picking', a feature for warehouse staff to pick, pack and send the orders. 'Create Order' feature UI/UX improvements. 'Specific Order' feature creation;

- lots of small UI/UX improvements;

- continuous work on Design System etc.
